Exam Lab Invigilator

What You'll Do

Job Responsibilities

As an exam lab invigilator, you play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity of computer-based examinations.

🪪

Verify Candidate Identities

Thoroughly check and verify the identity of each candidate before they enter the examination lab.

👁️

Monitor During Exams

Actively monitor candidates throughout the exam session to prevent malpractice and ensure focus.

📜

Enforce Exam Rules

Ensure all exam rules and guidelines are strictly followed by every candidate in the lab.

🛡️

Maintain Discipline

Uphold proper invigilation procedures and maintain a disciplined, quiet exam environment.
